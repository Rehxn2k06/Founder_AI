## Our Process

We run a tight, opinionated process that keeps projects from going sideways:

1. **Discovery (Week 0):** We dig into your users, the problem, the competitive landscape, and what "done" looks like. We come out of this with a prioritized feature list and a shared definition of the MVP.

2. **Design (Weeks 1–2):** Lo-fi wireframes → stakeholder review → hi-fi design system → prototypes. We design in the open — you're seeing work every 2 days, not at the end.

3. **Build (Weeks 3–8):** Two-week sprints. Working software ships at the end of every sprint. You have a staging environment from day one.

4. **Launch (Week 9–10):** Load testing, bug bash, App Store / Play Store submission or cloud deployment, handoff documentation, and a recorded walkthrough for your team.

5. **Post-launch:** We stick around for 30 days for critical bug fixes. After that, we offer retainer arrangements if you want us to keep the momentum going.
